How to Play

  • Goal: Don't be the one to complete a valid word!
  • Players take turns adding one letter at a time
  • Each letter must lead toward a valid word (3+ letters)
  • If you complete a valid word (3+ letters), you LOSE the round
  • Challenge: Think opponent's letters don't lead to a word? Challenge them!
  • If challenger is right, opponent loses. If wrong, challenger loses.
  • First to 5 points wins the game!
