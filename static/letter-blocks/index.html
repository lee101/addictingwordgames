How to Play

  • Form Words: Arrange falling letter blocks to spell words horizontally (left to right)
  • Clear Rows: Complete a valid word across the row to clear it and score points
  • Longer Words: Longer words (4+ letters) score bonus points!
  • Combos: Clear multiple rows at once for combo multipliers
  • Level Up: Clear lines to increase level and speed

Controls

  • ← → Move left/right
  • ↓ Soft drop
  • ↑ / Space Rotate piece
  • Enter Hard drop
  • P Pause game
